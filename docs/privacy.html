Privacy Policy

StickerSmash: yet another

Welcome to StickerSmash: yet another! This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

Overview

StickerSmash: yet another is designed with privacy in mind. Our app functions entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.

Information We Don't Collect

We want to be clear that StickerSmash does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track user behavior
  • Store user photos or edited images on external servers
  • Share any information with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking tools

Photo Library Access

StickerSmash requires access to your device's photo library solely to:

  • Load images you choose to edit
  • Save your edited images back to your photo library

All photo processing happens locally on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to any servers or shared with anyone.

Data Storage

All app operations occur locally on your device. Any images you create are saved directly to your device's photo library and remain under your complete control.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated revision date.
