Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add stickers to my photos?
    Select a photo from your gallery or use the default photo, then tap '+' button and choose a sticker from our collection. Drag to place the sticker, and double-tap to enlarge the sticker.
  • Can I save my edited photos?
    Yes! After customizing your photo, tap the save button to store it directly in your device's photo library.
  • What image formats are supported?
    StickerSmash supports JPEG and PNG formats for both input photos and saved results.
  • How do I select other photos?
    Simply the reset button, then the button to select a photo appears.

Getting Started

  • Download and install StickerSmash: yet another from the App Store
  • Grant photo access permission when prompted
  • Select a photo
  • Choose your favorite sticker
  • Save and share your creation!
